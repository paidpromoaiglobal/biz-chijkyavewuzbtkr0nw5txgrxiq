About Us

CE SPORTS

CE Sports is Jaipur's most trusted sports equipment store, proudly serving athletes and fitness enthusiasts from Vijay Nagar, Murlipura, Vidhyadhar Nagar, and across the city. Founded with a passion for sport, our store offers an extensive collection of cricket equipment, badminton rackets, sports footwear, workout apparel, and much more — all at honest, competitive prices. With a stellar 4.9-star rating from over 261 happy customers, we are the go-to destination for quality sporting goods in Rajasthan.

At CE Sports, we believe every player deserves the best gear without compromise. Our knowledgeable and friendly team, led by owner Monu Bhaiya, goes the extra mile to help you find exactly what you need — whether it's the perfect racket stringing, the right pair of shoes, or durable sportswear from top brands. We are open all 7 days a week from 9 AM to 10 PM so your sporting needs are always met, whenever they arise.
